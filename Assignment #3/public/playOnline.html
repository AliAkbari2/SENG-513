    How to play:
    Create a room with the generated room Id, or your own text input for the room Id. Enter your name, and choose number of players to play against.
    Copy the link and provide it to the number of player playing excluding you. Game will start once all remaining players have joined using the game room ID.
    Two or Three players take turns to join two adjacent dots with a horizontal or vertical line.
    If a player completes the fourth side of a box their initial is drawn on that box and they must draw another line.
    When all the boxes have been completed the winner is the player who has initialled the most boxes.
